My current mission is to empower people with the inner tools and resources needed for fulfilling lives. Many effective proven options available to realize this mission aren't well known or easy to apply in our current culture. My strategy leverages modern tactics and technology to make proven ideas more accessible and practical for everyone.

I pursue this mission through [Tools For Humanity](/tag/tools-for-humanity/) and [Workit](https://www.workitinc.com/).

I'm a full-stack maker, outdoor adventurer, and lifelong learner. I enjoy working with small startups where I can influence product, systems, and culture. My creative output spans product design, software development, hardware, 3D printing, and cooking. I share experiences through writing and speaking.

## Core Principles

1. **Bias to Action** – Learn through doing and iteration rather than waiting for perfect answers
2. **Be Intentional** – Maintain a clear vision and communicate transparently
3. **Diverge and Experiment** – Explore multiple perspectives and collaborate
4. **Be Curious** – Ask questions with a beginner's mindset
5. **Be Present** – Practice active listening and thoughtful engagement
6. **Be of Service** – Connect with and support others authentically
7. **Be Authentic and Vulnerable** – Bring your whole self to interactions
8. **Play** – Keep things light and joyful
